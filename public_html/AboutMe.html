About Me

Howdy! My name is Jasmine Leu and I’m from Plano, Texas, a suburb around Dallas. As a kid, I was involved in many diverse activities: Taekwondo, extracurricular math, Chinese, art, and swimming classes, a chess club, and piano lessons. I have lived in Plano for all my life except for when I temporarily moved to Taipei, Taiwan in 2011, where I attended an American private school (link). I moved back to Plano in 2013, where I stayed at until I graduated high school at Plano West Senior High School in 2018 alongside a class of 1400 students.


I like exploring new places, hiking, and just traveling in general. I love listening to music, especially when I’m coding, roadtripping, or just doing chores. My favorite genres are rock and pop-rock. I also know how to play a few instruments. My favorites are the piano and the flute; in fact, I’ve been playing the piano since I was 5 years old.

I absolutely adore animals - I could play with puppies and kittens all day, or merely watch a flock of ducks and ducklings for hours. My favorite animals are dogs and wolves, although I would really like to also have a kitten or a cat someday.



I have a Chihuahua named Rosie who, despite the stereotypes about Chihuahuas being loud and aggressive, is the sweetest dog I have ever met.
